Summary: How I decided to learn to code, and why I'm building in public.

This blog will be my learning journal. I'll share what I build, what breaks, and what I learn. No polished expert advice, just the real journey from zero to (hopefully) a freelance developer.

If you're also learning, or just curious, welcome. Let's see where this goes.
