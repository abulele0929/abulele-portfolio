Title: From the Pitch to the Keyboard
Date: 2026-06-19
Category: journey
Tags: sport science, coding, transition
Summary: How my background in sport science actually helps me write better code.

People ask me why I'm learning to code when I study sport science. The truth is, the two aren't that different. In sport science, you analyse data, look for patterns, and adjust your approach based on feedback. That's exactly what debugging feels like.

Today I'm working on a productivity app. It's a simple Python CLI tool that helps me track my study and coding time. The logic is straightforward, but I'm already learning about error handling and user input.

The keyboard is just another pitch.
